My name is Justin Ta. I'm 18 years old. My house is in the northeast section of Eastvale, where all the suburbs are, and I am not married. I am a student at University of California, Riverside, and I get home every day by 8 PM at the latest. I don't smoke, but I occasionally drink. I'm in bed by 10 PM, and make sure I get nine hours of sleep, no matter what. After having a glass of cold juice and doing about twenty minutes of browsing Reddit before going to bed, I usually have no problems sleeping until morning. Just like a baby, I wake up without any fatigue or stress in the morning. I was told there were no issues at my last check-up. I'm trying to explain that I'm a person who wishes to live a very quiet life. I take care not to trouble myself with any enemies, like winning and losing, that would cause me to lose sleep at night. That is how I deal with society, and I know that is what brings me happiness. Although, if I were to fight I wouldn't lose to anyone.
